# OPERA Property Management System Overview

## What is OPERA?

OPERA (Oracle Property Management System) is a comprehensive, enterprise-level property management system (PMS) developed by Oracle Hospitality. It's one of the world's most widely used hotel management platforms, serving hotels, resorts, casinos, cruise ships, and other hospitality properties of all sizes globally.

OPERA is the industry standard for managing hotel operations, from small boutique properties to large chain hotels and integrated resort complexes.

## What Does OPERA Do?

OPERA provides end-to-end hotel management functionality across all aspects of hospitality operations:

#### 1. **Reservations Management**
- Create, modify, and cancel reservations
- Individual and group bookings
- Wait list management
- Reservation routing and confirmation
- Rate management and room type assignment
- Package and promotion handling
- Deposit and guarantee processing

#### 2. **Front Office Operations**
- Guest check-in and check-out
- Room assignment and upgrades
- Key card management
- Guest messaging and communication
- Cashiering and payment processing
- Night audit automation
- Queue management for arrivals/departures

#### 3. **Guest Profile Management**
- Centralized guest database
- Preference tracking (room type, floor, amenities)
- Guest history and stay patterns
- VIP and loyalty program management
- Communication preferences

#### 4. **Housekeeping Management**
- Room status tracking (clean, dirty, inspected, out of order)

#### 5. **Rate Management**
- Dynamic pricing and yield management

#### 6. **Revenue Management**
- Demand forecasting

#### 7. **Sales and Catering**
- Group and corporate sales management
- Banquet and event management
- Function space management
- Catering orders and menus

- Contract management

#### 8. **Financial Management**
- Guest and non-guest billing
- Accounts receivable

- Tax calculation and compliance

#### 9. **Reporting and Analytics**
- Operational reports (occupancy, ADR, RevPAR)
- Financial reports (revenue, payments, aging)
- Forecasting reports
- Guest history and segmentation reports
- Performance dashboards
- Custom report builder
- Scheduled report distribution

#### 10. **Multi-Property Management**
- Central reservation system (CRS)
- Chain-wide guest profiles
- Cross-property bookings and transfers
- Consolidated reporting
- Enterprise-level rate management
- Loyalty program management across portfolio

### Core Workflows

#### Reservation to Check-Out Lifecycle

1. **Reservation Creation**
   - Availability check across room types
   - Rate calculation based on dates, market code, source
   - Guest profile creation or selection
   - Guarantee/payment method capture
   - Confirmation generation and distribution

2. **Pre-Arrival**
   - Arrival list generation
   - Pre-registration processing
   - Room blocking and assignment
   - Special request review
   - Pre-authorization of credit cards

3. **Check-In**
   - Guest verification (ID, payment method)
   - Room key encoding
   - Folio creation
   - Registration card generation
   - Upselling opportunities presentation
   - Welcome messaging

4. **In-House**
   - Folio management (charges, payments, adjustments)
   - Guest requests and service orders
   - Housekeeping status updates
   - Incidental charges posting (F&B, spa, etc.)
   - Guest communications
   - Loyalty point accrual

5. **Check-Out**
   - Folio review and settlement
   - Payment processing
   - Departure status update
   - Room status change to dirty
   - Late charges posting (if applicable)
   - Feedback collection
   - Receipt generation

#### Night Audit Process

**Automated end-of-day procedure that:**
- Rolls over the business date
- Posts recurring charges (room and tax)
- Processes scheduled tasks
- Generates end-of-day reports
- Backs up transaction data
- Reconciles all financial transactions
- Updates occupancy statistics
- Creates audit trail
